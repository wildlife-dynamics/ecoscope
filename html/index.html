Ecoscope

A free and open-source data analytics and reporting platform designed to turn conservation data into action.

Log in Sign up

Insights in minutes, not weeks

Conservation teams collect more data than ever, but turning it into insight is still a struggle. Ecoscope cuts time spent wrangling spreadsheets, bridges the gap between data collection and decision-making, and frees up more time for the field.

What Ecoscope offers conservationists

Web App

Run workflows in your browser, with nothing to install. Pull in raw data, run in-depth analyses, and generate visualizations that reveal patterns in minutes — so teams can monitor trends and spot risks at a glance.

Desktop App

Run Ecoscope locally, on your own hardware. Primarily intended for developers.

Help Center

Learn to use the apps and troubleshoot issues.

Build Your Own

For advanced users: create and run custom analytical workflows from Ecoscope's pre-built components.

Community

Join the conversation. Ask questions, share workflows, and connect with other Ecoscope users.

Export Data

Export EarthRanger data directly from Ecoscope Web or Desktop to start your analysis your own way.

Made possible by collaboration

Ecoscope was first envisioned by Dr. Jake Wall in the effort to understand and protect African elephants. It’s a free and open-source platform led by Wildlife Dynamics and EarthRanger, supported by Allen Family Philanthropies, Mara Elephant Project, Save the Elephants, and Elephants Alive.
